House Price Prediction

Using Machine Learning




How It Works



A user can write the "Area (in square feet)", select the number of "BHK" and "Bathroom", and at last, choose the

preferred "Location" and click on the "Predict" button. After clicking, the aforementioned ML model will predict the price of the house as

per the data submitted by the user.



About

To predict the price of House in a particular area purely based on machine learning algorithm. This can be used by the buyer as well as the seller for estimation which is purely based on the data without any intervention from brokers and commissions.
